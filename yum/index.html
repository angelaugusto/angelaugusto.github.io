Red Headed

Stranger

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces wild turkey bourbon
  • 1 ounce domaine de canton
  • 2 pinches cayenne pepper
  • squeeze of lemon
  • 2 dashes angostura bitters

Preparation

  1. 1.Add 2 pinches Cayenne, lemon, and bitters to shaker glass, stir to dissolve Cayenne.
  2. 2.Add Wild Turkey and Domaine de Canton.
  3. 3.Shake ingredients over ice until cold.
  4. 4.Strain into ice-filled rocks glass.
  5. 5.Garnish with candied ginger and enjoy.
